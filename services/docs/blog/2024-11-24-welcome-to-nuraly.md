# Welcome to Nuraly Documentation

We're excited to introduce the Nuraly platform - a modern full-stack application platform built with microservices architecture.

## What is Nuraly?

Nuraly is designed to help developers build better applications faster. Our platform provides:

- **Microservices Architecture** - Modular, scalable services
- **Docker Compose Orchestration** - Simplified development and deployment
- **Built-in Authentication** - Integrated Keycloak for identity management
- **Developer-Friendly** - Hot reload, automatic migrations, and comprehensive tooling
